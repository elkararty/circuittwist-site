Circuit Twist — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Circuit Twist ("the app") is developed by Waleed Elkararty ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data is handled when you play.

Data we collect ourselves

None. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and no servers of our own. Your progress (stars, scores, streaks, settings) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the app shows ads served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). To serve and measure ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect information from your device, including:

  • IP address (used to estimate general location);
  • Device identifiers, such as the advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt;
  • Ad interaction data (such as which ads you have seen or tapped);
  • Non-user-related crash logs and performance data.

If you decline the tracking prompt, ads still appear but are contextual rather than personalized. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Google's handling of this data is described in Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data in AdMob.

Removing ads

A one-time in-app purchase ("Remove Ads") disables all advertising. The purchase is processed entirely by Apple; we receive no personal or payment information. After purchase, the ad SDK no longer serves ads in the app.

Children

The app is a general-audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. We do not collect personal information from anyone; advertising data handling is described above.
