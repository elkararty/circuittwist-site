Common questions

I bought Remove Ads but still see ads.
Open Settings (gear icon) → tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed into the same Apple Account you bought it with.

My progress disappeared.
Progress is stored on your device. Deleting the app deletes progress — reinstalling starts fresh. (Restoring your phone from a backup restores it.)

A level seems impossible.
Every level is generated with a guaranteed solution — try the hint button (💡) to highlight the next tile that needs turning. Watch for special tiles: ↺ spins backwards, 🔗 twins turn together, numbered tiles have limited spins.

How do dual circuits work?
Boards with two bolts have two separate circuits. Connect each bolt to the power ring of the same color — both must be live at once.
